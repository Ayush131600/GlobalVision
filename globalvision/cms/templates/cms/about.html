Born From The Trails

We started because a trekker showed up in Pokhara with just a backpack, big dreams, and absolutely zero gear suited for the Annapurna Circuit. He was excited but completely unprepared.

We realized then that finding high-quality, trustworthy gear in town could be overwhelming. You shouldn't have to compromise on safety or comfort when you traverse some of the highest peaks on Earth.

The Local Guide Angle: We aren't just a rental shop behind a desk. We are locals. We’ve hiked every single trail we rent equipment for. We test the boots on rocky ascents, and we sleep in the sleeping bags at freezing base camps. This way, we know exactly what you need to stay safe and enjoy the journey.

"We test the gear so you don't have to face surprises at 4,000 meters."

Our Mission & Values

Safety First

We rigorously clean and inspect every piece of gear upon return, ensuring it is 100% reliable for your next high-altitude challenge.

Sustainability

We proudly support local porters, enforce eco-trekking principles, and ensure our rental processes reduce waste in the Himalayas.

Simplicity

Rent online, pick it up upon arrival, and hit the trails. We strip away the complicated logistics so you can focus on the climb.

Why Pokhara?

Situated beside the tranquil Phewa Lake with the majestic Annapurna range reflecting in its waters, Pokhara is the undeniable gateway to the Himalayas. It’s a city where adrenaline meets serenity.

From paragliding to world-class trekking circuits, resting here before or after your journey isn't just convenient—it's essential. We established Global Vision here to provide ground-level support right where your physical journey begins.
