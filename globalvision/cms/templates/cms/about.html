We are Global Vision.

A Pokhara-based team dedicated to providing the best equipment and local knowledge for Himalayan adventurers.

Our Story

Global Vision started in 2020 with a simple goal: to make high-quality trekking gear accessible to everyone visiting Pokhara. We saw that many adventurers were struggling with substandard equipment, and we wanted to change that.

Since then, we've helped hundreds of trekkers and explorers reach their goals safely. We've expanded from simple trekking bags to high-end vehicles and specialized equipment, always maintaining our focus on quality and reliability.

Why Choose Us?

Reliability

Every piece of gear is personally checked before every rental to ensure your safety and comfort.

Local Expertise

We've hiked these trails ourselves and can offer real advice on what you'll actually need.

Fair Pricing

Premium gear at honest, local prices. No hidden costs or tourist-only markups.
